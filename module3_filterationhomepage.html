About Water Filtration

Water filtration is a crucial process for ensuring clean and safe drinking water. Our filtration system uses the CTF (Coagulation, Treatment, Filtration) process to effectively remove impurities and contaminants. The process involves coagulation to clump impurities, treatment to neutralize contaminants, and filtration to remove them from the water.

Coagulation

The first step in the filtration process where chemicals are added to water to cause contaminants to clump together, making them easier to remove.

Treatment

This step involves adding chemicals that neutralize contaminants, making them easier to remove during the filtration process.

Filtration

The final step where water passes through filters to remove coagulated particles and impurities, ensuring clean and safe drinking water.
